# Credits

## Art — Ninja Adventure Asset Pack

The Yosemite valley's pixel art is built on the **Ninja Adventure Asset Pack** by
**pixel-boy** (with AAA), released under **Creative Commons Zero (CC0)**.

- Pack: https://pixel-boy.itch.io/ninja-adventure-asset-pack
- Author: https://pixel-boy.itch.io/
- License: CC0 (attribution not required, but given here with thanks)

Attribution is not legally required under CC0, but it is included here and surfaced in the
in-valley "Under the hood" (The Ahwahnee) modal as a thank-you.

Assets used (cherry-picked subset in `public/game/ninja-adventure/`): the floor/grass,
nature (trees/rocks), water, relief (cliffs), house, and camp tilesets; the Hunter character
sprite sheet + faceset (the hiker); and the Fog / Raylight environment FX. The three Yosemite
landmarks the pack cannot provide (El Capitan, Half Dome, Yosemite Falls) are assembled from
pack tiles plus minimal custom pixels authored in the pack's 16-px style and palette.
